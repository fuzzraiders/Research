# 📝 About

Welcome to the **FuzzRaiders Research** repository. This space hosts **public, safe, and ethically responsible** cybersecurity research produced by the FuzzRaiders team.

Our research spans vulnerability analysis, exploit behavior studies, protocol investigations, and emerging threat trends.

# 🎯 Purpose

- Publish high-quality, public-safe research
- Document analysis methods & reverse engineering workflows
- Share sanitized PoC demonstrations
- Provide insights into modern attack surfaces
- Support defensive and educational security efforts

## 📂 Repository Structure

```
/research
├── web/
├── cloud/
├── malware/
├── reverse-engineering/
├── protocol-analysis/
├── cryptography/
├── exploit-case-studies/
├── threat-landscape/
└── misc/

```

Each folder includes topic-specific studies and a README.

## 📘 Types of Research

### 🔹 Vulnerability Analysis

Deep-dives into publicly disclosed weaknesses.

### 🔹 Reverse Engineering Notes

Behavioral investigations using safe, legal samples.

### 🔹 Malware & Threat Intelligence (Sanitized)

Analysis of TTPs, actor behaviors, and ecosystem trends.

### 🔹 Exploit Case Studies

Exploration of exploit techniques and mitigation strategies.

### 🔹 Safe PoCs

Redacted, non-destructive demonstrations for education.

### 🔹 Research Papers

Formal PDF reports, diagrams, and methodology write-ups.
